<"color:white;">Stephen Lucas, Lead Creative Progammer for Composition Studies at UNT created this game and programmed the code in html, css, and javascript. I took the game and decided to make it in the style of an indie Japanese RPG maker game. The images come from a horror RPG game called Fear and Hunger. The multitude of sounds, which I gathered from a website, reminded me of the sounds I would hear from the RPG maker games I played. The ball will speed up every time it hits one of the paddles and reset after a point is scored. Please use W and S to control the left paddle; the up and down arrows to control the right paddle. Have fun becoming horrifically anxious!
